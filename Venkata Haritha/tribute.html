  • 24 December 1892: At Kanyakumari, South India
  • 13 February 1893: First public lecture, Secunderabad, South India
  • 31 May 1893: Sails for America from Mumbai
  • 30 July 1893: Arrives in Chicago
  • 11 September 1893: First address at Parliament of Religions, Chicago
  • 16 May 1894: Speaks at Harvard University
  • 1895: Forms Vedanta Society of New York and begins classes in New York
  • October-November 1895: Lectures in London
  • 30 December 1896: Leaves Naples for India
  • 19 February 1897: Arrives in Kolkata
  • 1 May 1897: Establishes Ramakrishna Mission Association, Kolkata
  • 9 December 1898: Consecrates Belur Math
  • 19 March 1899: Establishes Advaita Ashrama at Mayavati
  • 20 June 1899: Leaves India for second visit to the West
  • 22 February 1900: Arrives in San Francisco
  • 14 April 1900: Founds Vedanta Society in San Francisco
  • 26 November 1900: Leaves for India
  • 9 December 1900: Arrives at Belur Math
  • 4 July 1902: Death at the age of 39
